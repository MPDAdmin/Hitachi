  • COVID-19

 

During these unusual times we at Hitachi are drawing upon our founding spirit and values to respond to the challenges that coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic has brought.

We recognize that many of our customers are operating in industries that are critical to the production and delivery of medical, pharmaceutical, food, beverage and personal care products.

We would like to assure you that our production will be maintained, and we will continue to maintain the supply chain of essential products and provide support to all of our customers and our partners.

The health and safety of all our associates and their families remains our top priority and in response to the health authorities’ recommendations, our teams will be working remotely. But will continue to provide direct on-site support where essential, within the health and safety guidelines, to meet your essential needs.

At Hitachi, we and our associates are committed to being the partner you can rely upon in the days, months and years ahead – in both good times and difficult ones.

The COVID-19 situation remains fluid and our leadership team meet regularly to navigate our way through these uncharted waters. And together, we will see this through.
