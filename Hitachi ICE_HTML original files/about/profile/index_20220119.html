Company Profile

Industrial Components and Equipment Group (ICE) provides the most appropriate solution based on various product development, engineering, sales and after-service skills. The group is responsible for the marketing of various industrial products such as Air Compressor, Ink Jet Printer, Inverter, Induction Motor, Switches, Transformer, Hoist, and various energy saving equipments.

Our market coverage includes Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, Philippines, Thailand, Myanmar, Cambodia & Vietnam (South East Asia), India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka & Nepal (South West Asia) and UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, Egypt, Jordan, Syria, Lebanon & Bahrain (Middle East Countries).
