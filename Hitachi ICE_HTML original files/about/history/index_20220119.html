History

Hitachi established as a repair shop at a mining site in 1910, and started its business as a manufacturer, producing the first "5HP Induction Motor".

Over 100 years of operation, Hitachi developed a broad variety of products, services and solutions that addressed the wide range of challenges faced by the society. With environmental issues like global warming becoming more and more urgent in recent years, Hitachi has been researching energy efficiency and green energy to improve the global environments and is developing related equipment and systems while refining technology by applying developed products to the company's own plants.

The company aims to contribute to the improvement of current and future global, social and security environments using its technological capabilities.
