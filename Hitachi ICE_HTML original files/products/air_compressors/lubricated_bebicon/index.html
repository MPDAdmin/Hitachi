Lubricated Bebicon

Model Nomenclature

 

The Hitachi lubricated ( oil-flooded ) Bebicon compressor is a heavy duty piston compressor designed to operate long hours. Suitable for manufacturing industries, automotive service workshops, spray painting and pneumatic tools.

  • Features
  • Specifications
  • Brochures & Enquiry

Features

Compressor Head

  • High performance. High reliability. Compact and light. Easy to maintain.

Good Cooling Design

  • The cylinder head uses an aluminum alloy and ventilated rib to improve heat radiation and air capacity.
  • The v-groove design is located between the discharge and suction chamber to reduce the heat transfer and improve air capacity.
