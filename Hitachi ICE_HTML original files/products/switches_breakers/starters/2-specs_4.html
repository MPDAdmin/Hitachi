Note

*1
Application of Category AC3 and AC2 to the reversible electromagnetic starters shall be limited to regular reversible operation in which a motor starts reverse rotation after it has once stopped. Category AC4 is applicable when the motor starts reverse rotation before it has completely stops. And the contactors used for reversible operation, they must be electrically interlocked using by mutual NC auxiliary contacts.
