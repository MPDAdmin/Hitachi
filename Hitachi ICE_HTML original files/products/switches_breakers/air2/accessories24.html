Air Circuit Breakers [AKH, AKS, AKN Series] : Accessories : Pad Lock/Position Lock [PL]

ACB is subject to restriction regarding moving in connected, test, disconnected when drawing in or out. If main body of ACB is placed in 3 positions, it is locked and stopped when drawing in or out.

  • As shown in the figure, if draw-in / out button pops out, it means locking is operating.
  • To continue Draw-in / out operation, release lock by pushing Draw-in / out button
  • In case it is locked as shown in the figure above, main body of ACB can not be drawn in or out into the cradle.
  • For the lock device, user has to purchase it. (φ5 – φ6)
