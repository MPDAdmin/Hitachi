Motors

Hitachi, Ltd. was initially founded in 1910 as an electric repair shop. In 1914, Hitachi became the first manufacturer to manufacture industrial electric motors in Japan. Today, Hitachi manufactures state-of-the-art motors and motor products ranging from the smallest micro motors to the largest motor available anywhere in the world.
