TL Type Toprun Type Crane Saddle

  • Features
  • Specifications
  • Saddle Selection
  • Brochures & Enquiry

Features

  1. By the inverted hat-section structure () that is less torsional deformation, and the integral structure of the frame, inspection and change of the wheel, manufacturing of the crane girder will be easy.
  2. Brake torque can be adjusted widely from 0% to 60% of the motor torque, and it is user-friendly.
  3. Hitachi suspension type crane saddle is produced on the premise of I-beam is used as girder.
