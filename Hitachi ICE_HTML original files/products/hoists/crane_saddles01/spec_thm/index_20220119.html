THM Type

Toprun Type <Trackless> Crane Saddle

Hitachi trackless trolley is the latest model that the latest technology is applied to our experiences. Polyurethane Rubber is used for the wheels. It is silent and smooth, and thus it is suitable for a factory that is located near a residential area. Reliability of the saddle has been improved also.

  • Brake torque can be adjusted widely from 0% to 60% of the motor torque, and it is user-friendly.
  • Manufacturing time of the crane can be reduced by the centering mark for adjustment.
  • Driving side of the wheel part is with a gear cover.

Features

  • Noise is reduced by approx. 10dB (A-characteristics) by the polyurethane wheel in comparison to the crane with flanged wheel.
  • As wheels run smoothly and polyurethane absorb the vibration of the hoist, vibration conducted by the building will be reduced.
  • As traveling rail is the H-beam only, and costs of the rail and manufacturing are not required, the total cost can be reduced when length of the traveling rail is more than 20 meters. (Estimation by Hitachi)
