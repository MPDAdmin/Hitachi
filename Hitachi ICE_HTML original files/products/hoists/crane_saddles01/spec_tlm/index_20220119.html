TLM Type

Toprun Type <Trackless> Crane Saddle

The new TLM type toprun type trackless crane saddle is a sister product of the THM type saddle that economic efficiency is pursued, and suitable for light to medium load conditions. In accordance with the trend of bigger building and heavier load, maximum wheel load 10 ton and 15 ton (made to order) classes of the trackless crane saddles are lined up. You can select the most suitable trackless trolley from a variety of models
