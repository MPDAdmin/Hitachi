Metalex Thailand 2018 held in Bangkok, Thailand (21st – 24th November 2018)

“METALEX” continues a strong reputation as ASEAN’s Metalworking Metropolis in this 32nd edition. With 99,998 quality visitors during 21st – 24th November who came from all over the region and beyond, to network and explore industry 4.0 technologies, machinery and solutions for metalworking from 3,300 brands from 50 countries, 9 international pavilions.

Hitachi Asia (Thailand) Co., Ltd. executives and staff gathered to officially celebrate their participation at METALEX 2018. The ribbon cutting ceremony was performed by the company’s General Manager, Mr Seiji Shinozaki with a memorable remark that METALEX is the largest metalworking event in Southeast Asia. Hence, the company decided to join this edition to present Hitachi Asia’s high-quality solution and motor to support the region’s manufacturing factories.
